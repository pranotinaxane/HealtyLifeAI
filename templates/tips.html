Wellness Pillars

Our recommendations are based on six structural areas of lifestyle health.

1. Nutrition

Focus on balanced home-cooked Indian meals, whole grains, and lean proteins.

2. Activity

Combine daily brisk walking, stretching, and resistance/strength training.

3. Hydration

Aim for a baseline of 2-3+ liters of pure water depending on your body weight.

4. Rest

Secure 7-9 hours of dark, cool room sleep to allow cellular repair cycles.

5. Mindset

Incorporate micro-meditations, breathing sets, and digital detox habits daily.

6. Hygiene

Maintain screen limits, clean breathing air, and structural spinal alignment.
