Pro Hydration Tips

Morning Kickstart

Drink a full glass of water right after waking up to rehydrate your body after sleep and stimulate digestion.

Set Reminders

Keep a water bottle beside your desk. Drink small sips continuously instead of gulping large quantities at once.

Color Test

Check your urine color. Light straw or pale yellow indicates proper hydration. Dark yellow signifies you need to drink water immediately.
